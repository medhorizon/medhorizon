# MedHorizon

MedHorizon is an AI creation workspace for medical and scientific storytelling, built for new learners who want strong results without needing expert-level prompts. Inspired by the clarity and ambition of products like Claude Science, MedHorizon focuses on helping people move from idea to polished output through feasible workflows, practical parameter choices, and repeatable creative guidance.

Instead of expecting users to already know the right prompt structure, model settings, or generation pipeline, MedHorizon aims to discover them for you. The goal is simple: lower the skill barrier, surface workflows that actually work, and make good-looking, high-quality outputs easier to achieve from the start.

## English

### What MedHorizon Is

MedHorizon is designed for beginners, educators, researchers, and creators who want beautiful and useful outputs without spending weeks learning prompt engineering. Users should not need professional prompts to do strong work. MedHorizon will help identify feasible workflows and effective parameters for the task at hand, so the path to a compelling result is clearer and more reliable.

### Core Idea

- You bring the goal, not a perfect prompt.
- MedHorizon finds a workable process.
- MedHorizon suggests practical parameters and generation settings.
- MedHorizon helps turn rough intent into refined, visually strong output.

### Vision

We want creative and scientific AI tools to feel accessible, not intimidating. MedHorizon is built around the belief that beginners deserve systems that can guide them toward good decisions, not just systems that reward already-expert users.

## 中文

### MedHorizon 是什么

MedHorizon 是一个面向医学与科学内容创作的 AI 工作空间，重点服务新手学习者、教育者、研究者与内容创作者。它的目标不是要求用户先学会“专业 Prompt 写法”，而是帮助用户在创作过程中自动找到可行的工作流、合适的参数设置，以及更容易产出高质量、好看结果的方法。

### 核心理念

- 你只需要提出目标，而不必先写出专业 Prompt。
- MedHorizon 会帮助寻找可行、稳定的工作流程。
- MedHorizon 会推荐更合适的参数与生成设置。
- MedHorizon 会把模糊想法逐步推进成更完整、更美观、更有表现力的输出。

### 愿景

我们希望 AI 创作工具对新手同样友好，而不是只对已经熟悉提示词工程的人有效。MedHorizon 的核心价值，是降低门槛、提升结果质量，让更多人能够更快地做出优秀且美观的作品。
